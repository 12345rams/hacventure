Cyber laws provide legal recognition to electronic documents and a framework to support e-filing and e-commerce transactions. They also provide a legal framework to mitigate and check cyber crimes 1. The Information Technology Act 2000 (IT Act 2000) is an example of a cyber law in India. Cyber law also provides legal protections to people using the internet, both businesses and everyday citizens3. Cyber law is a new and evolving area of law that adapts to the changing technologies and challenges of the digital world. Cyber law covers a wide range of topics, such as fraud, copyright, defamation, harassment, freedom of speech, and cybercrime
